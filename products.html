Products and Services

UCPL current products portfolio is made up of Cocoa Power products produced from cocoa cake into fine powder for industrial and domestic use under the brand names; “BROWNCO” (domestic use) and “MASTA BRAND” (industrial use). These products were approved by Ghana Food and Drugs Authority (FDA) for distribution in the domestic and export markets. The company also process Cocoa Butter and Cocoa Liquor under a tolling arrangement with cocoa processing companies to meet the demand of its export clients
