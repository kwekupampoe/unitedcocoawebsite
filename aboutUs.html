Our Story

United Cocoa Product Limited was established on April 27th, 2006, under the Companies Act code 1963 act 176. We've grown to become a leading force in cocoa processing, with a current production capacity of 3,600 metric tons of natural and alkalized cocoa powder for both local and international markets.
