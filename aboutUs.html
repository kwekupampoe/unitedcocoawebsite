About Us

United Cocoa product limited is a limited liability company incorporated on 27th April, 2006 under the Companies Act code 1963 act 176 with the mandate to engage in cocoa processing import, and export activities. The company currently produces 3,600 metric tons of natural and alkalized cocoa powder for the local and international market. The company intends to start producing from the cocoa bean stage throught to powder and confectionery products
