Cocoa Mass / Liquor

Cocoa Mass / Liquor

Produced from the finest cocoa beans on Earth (Ghana Cocoa) into a thick dark brown paste derived from the deshelled and roasted cocoa beans. Benefits of Liquor: it is linked to improved blood flow, reduced blood pressure and enhanced vascular function.

Cocoa Butter

Fat obtained from cocoa beans used in manufacture of confectionery and cosmetic product. This pale yellow, edible fat extraction can be used to make chocolate. Benefits of cocoa butter: improve blood flow to the skin and slow aging by protecting from the sun's harmful UV rays. Cocoa butter is used to smoothen scars, wrinkles and other marks on the skin.

Cocoa Powder

It is a dark-brown powder used to make chocolate. This is the powdery remains of chocolate liquor after cocoa butter is removed. It is used in baking and low in fat (10 – 12%) and low-calorie recipes as a flavouring for ice cream and beverage industry. Its scientific name – Theobroma Cacao, which translates to "food of the gods". The ancient Mayans and Aztecs used Cacao as medicine for centuries.
